About Eros

We’re here for the laughs you don’t stage, the tears you don’t plan, and the tiny in-between moments that make the day yours. Our approach is simple: keep it real, keep it beautiful, and keep it present so you can stay in the moment.

Based in Harlingen and across the Rio Grande Valley, we blend documentary candor with a clean, editorial finish. Expect gentle direction when it helps, unobtrusive coverage when it matters, pro lighting when needed, and optional drone for scale. We’re local to the RGV—and gladly travel.
